Little Heist

  • How To Play

    Collect coins to advance.
    Don't get caught by those pesky guards.

    Level 1 - 10 coins
    Level 2 - 20 coins
    Level 3 - 30 coins


  • Controllers

    To START press Return/Enter
    To RESTART press A
    To PAUSE press the Spacebar
